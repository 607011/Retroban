How to play

Use the arrow keys to move the player.

Push the boxes onto the goals to win. The fewer moves, the better.

Press R to restart the level.

Press U or Z to undo the last step.

Press N or . to go to the next level.

Press P or , to go to the previous level.

Press C to change the collection.

Press A to toggle player's animation.

Press S to show the solution.

Press H or ? to show this help dialog.
